Title: Why I Built a Film Recommendation Engine That Works Offline

Recently, I launched Show Suggesterâ€”an open-source tool for discovering films you'll actually love. Here's why it matters.

**The Problem with Modern Recommendations**

Every major streaming platform uses collaborative filtering (what millions of people watched) combined with engagement optimization (what keeps you watching longer). The result? Recommendations that are optimized to make you *addicted*, not *satisfied*.

**A Better Approach: Content-Based Filtering**

Show Suggester takes a different approach. It analyzes the *content* of films you've loved and finds similar ones:
- Genre overlap
- Director/cast matches
- Year/era proximity
- Runtime preferences

It's transparent (you can see why something's recommended), honest (no engagement manipulation), and effective.

**The Privacy Advantage**

All recommendations run locally in your browser. Your ratings never leave your device. No tracking. No profile building. No data selling.
